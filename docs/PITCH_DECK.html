Root Cause

Why Patch Management Fails

🔥
Too Much Noise
CVSS scores rank everything as critical. With thousands of high-severity CVEs, nothing is actually prioritized. Teams spend more time triaging than patching.
Result: Teams patch the loudest vulns,
not the riskiest ones.
🏗️
No Business Context
CVEs are technical artifacts. Executives speak in risk, compliance, and business outcomes. There's no shared language between the security team and leadership.
Result: "We patched 300 CVEs" means
nothing in a board meeting.
🤚
Broken Workflows
Security finds vulnerabilities. IT patches them. No shared tooling, no common language, no handoff process. Tickets get lost. Windows get missed. Audits get failed.
Result: Blame travels faster
than patches do.
These aren't people problems. They're tooling and process problems — and they're solvable.
